Who We Are

The Akatsuki (ÊöÅ, Akatsuki, Literally meaning: Dawn) is a criminal organization in the Naruto universe. It is led by Pain and was formed with the intention of bringing peace to the world by destroying the villages and ruling over the world. The Akatsuki is composed of former members of the Hidden Villages, who were either banished or ostracized for their actions. The Akatsuki members are known for their unique abilities and for wearing distinctive clothing. The Akatsuki is one of the main antagonistic organizations in the series.
